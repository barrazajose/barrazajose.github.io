A Message from the Director

Dra. Araceli Celina Justo López
School of Engineering Director

Welcome to the website of the Engineering Faculty of the Autonomous University of Baja California.

The Engineering Faculty began its activities in 1967. Our mission is to fully train professionals in the area of engineering at the undergraduate and postgraduate level, complying with the best standards of educational quality, being capable of providing optimal solutions to problems in the field of development, in harmony with university values and seeking our society well-being. In addition, carry out basic and applied research and promote technological innovation and industry linkage.

Currently, it offers 11 educational programs: Bachelor in Computer Systems, Computer Engineer, Electronics Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, Electrical Engineer, Mechatronic Engineer, Civil Engineer, Industrial Engineer, Bioengineer, Aerospace Engineer and Renewable Energy Engineer.

Our teaching staff is highly trained to offer quality and high value education. It has research professors who generate new knowledge in some engineering fields to solve problems that contribute to society development.

Industry linkages are promoted. This allows students to carry out innovative projects in a real work environment, thus facilitating their job placement.

Aware of the importance of comprehensive training for university students, we promote conferences, events, workshops and some activities are continuously organized to seek and encourage students' interest in science development, art, culture, sports and environmental care. Students are encouraged to participate in innovation, entrepreneurship and technological development competitions, which allow them to apply and increase their creativity and entrepreneurial spirit.

To answer questions or expand this information, follow us in our social networks or use our contact information.
