Bioengineering Program

MISSION

To train competent engineers in the biomedical and bio-industrial areas. Responsible, creative, supportive, participatory with an entrepreneurial, innovative and leadership attitude. Engineers committed to society, able to communicate effectively both orally and in writing topics related to their profession; to generate equipment and instruments for biomedical and biotechnological use; to incorporate and integrate technological and information systems for biomedical and bio-industrial use. Engineers that acknowledge and practice continuous updates in their area of specialty; employing scientific, technological, humanistic, and management knowledge with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies according to current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and to insure the quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, contributing to a sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for companies and the health sector, and improving the quality of life for the local, state, regional, national, and international population.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2030, the Bioengineer program is a widely recognized program, dynamic and committed to society in the generation of professionals, trained in an environment of high scientific and technological quality in a high civic and ethical level in which students respond assertively to situations in their personal, social and professional life through active participation in teaching processes and innovative research in the bioengineering area.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admission Profile

Applicants to enter the Bioengineer educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Physics
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Computer science
  • English

Skills:

  • Organization
  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Study habits
  • Teamwork

Attitudes:

  • Interest in continuous learning
  • Community service
  • Work Discipline
  • Sense of order
  • Proactive

Core Values:

  • Respect for life and the environment
  • Respect for their peers and the institution
  • Honesty and commitment

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, Bioengineers should be able to:

  • Design and generate equipment, devices and materials for biomedical, biotechnological and environmental use through the application of theoretical-practical foundations of bioengineering and international quality standards to achieve continuous improvement of technological and economic resources for health sector companies. Demonstrate collaborative attitude and social responsibility with emphasis to the environment well-being.
  • Propose and manage physical spaces for biomedical technological and computer systems. Applying bioengineering techniques and tools to biomedical, biotechnological and industrial functions in accordance to current regulations in order to contribute to quality care in the health field and the safety in industrial processes, with honesty and attitude towards interdisciplinary work.
  • Design and implement strategies for the generation of bioprocesses through fundamentals, techniques, bioengineering methods and biotechnological resources for the prevention and resolution of environmental problems and to contribute to sustainable development with an innovative, committed and tolerant attitude.
  • Manage human, material and financial resources in the design, manufacture, marketing, equipment of bioengineering products and services through the application of administrative processes, to contribute to the development of organizations and the creation of new productive spaces, with leadership, ethics, entrepreneurship, creativity and responsibility.

Educational objectives

  • Promote sustainable development through bioengineering innovation with professionals in design and interdisciplinary projects management that address productive and social sector problems.
  • Bioengineering professionals are capable of identifying opportunities and improve their skills to work in emerging areas of society and the productive sector and contribute to the economic development of the region and the country.
  • Contribute to the manufacturing systematization of commercial processes of products for biotechnological and biomedical purposes with bioengineers highly trained to meet the industrial and health care sector’s needs.

Occupational Field

Bioengineers may work in:

Private sector:

  • Health care sector
  • Educational sector
  • Research and development departments
  • Medical equipment and materials manufacturing industry
  • Biotechnology industry
  • Environmental industry
  • Service companies

Public Sector:

  • Health sector
  • Regulatory bodies
  • Educational institutions and research centers
  • Government agencies
  • In environmental improvement and the use of natural resources
  • In organizations that promote agricultural development
  • Other agencies and entities at the federal, state and municipal levels

Independent professionals:

  • Advice and training of personnel in the area of bioengineering
  • Provides advisory services and training of personnel in the bioengineering area
  • Runs its own company in the Bioengineering field
