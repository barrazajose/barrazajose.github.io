Industrial Engineering Program

MISSION

To train autonomous professionals in quality, production and manufacturing areas, who are competent at local, national, and international levels. Capable of solving problems with a multidisciplinary approach that contributes to the competitiveness of the goods and services industry with environmental awareness, responsibility and ethical commitment, as well as having the ability to generate knowledge and relevant technological innovations committed to the values of the human being.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2025, the Industrial Engineer educational program will have a regional, national and international recognition for the high-quality training of its graduates, positioned itself as the leading higher educational program teaching industrial engineering. This will be achieved through a consolidated academic plant, teaching practice, research, generation of scientific knowledge impacting the quality of life of Baja California citizen. In addition to promoting a commitment to the well-being and conservation of the environment.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Identify, formulate and solve complex engineering problems applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze, and synthesize engineering design processes that result in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Develop and conduct adequate experimentation; analyze and interpret data using engineering judgment to draw conclusions.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize their ethical and professional responsibilities in relevant situations in order to make informed judgments considering the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and social context.
  • Recognize the continuing need for additional knowledge and have the ability to locate, evaluate, integrate and apply this knowledge appropriately.
  • Work effectively in teams to set goals, plan tasks, meet deadlines and analyze risks.

Educational objectives

  • Design, manage and optimize processes, products and services
  • Generate and analyze data
  • Develop management systems
  • Integrate and lead teamwork

  4. Organizational culture analysis.

  6. 3D scanner validation for characterization of replacement parts.
