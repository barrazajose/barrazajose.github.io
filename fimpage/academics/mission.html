About Us

Training professionals in the engineering field as an undergraduate and postgraduate level, ...

Mission

To train engineering professionals at the undergraduate and graduate level in a comprehensive manner, capable of facing the challenges of their profession with leadership, entrepreneurial attitude and ethical commitment who contribute in an innovative, critical and creative way to the advancement of science, technology and to a sustainable development in the regional, cross-border, national and international spheres.

Vision

In 2030 the Mexicali Engineering Faculty is a national benchmark with international recognition for the quality of its undergraduate and graduate educational programs characterized by educational inclusion and equity, the humanistic vision and competitiveness of its graduates, and the excellence of its academics. Research, extension and linkage activities with the productive and social sector contribute widely to the development of science and technology for the benefit of society.

Values

The School of Engineering shares UABC fundamental values as a result of a survey conducted through the school community, the following results were obtained:

  • Honesty
  • Trust
  • Humility
  • Democracy
  • Loyalty
  • Solidarity
  • Respect
  • Responsability
  • Justice
  • Freedom
  • Perseverance
