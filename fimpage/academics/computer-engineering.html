Computer Engineering Program

MISSION

To train human resources with values, competent in national and international spheres, creative and entrepreneurial; capable of generating, applying and disseminating knowledge and good practices of their discipline to contribute to the technological, economic and social development of Baja California.

Engineers that recognize and practice continuous updating in their specialization area, using scientific knowledge, technological, humanistic in a higher position with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies.

According with current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and insurance of quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for health sector companies.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

  • Computer knowledge

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students will be able to:

  • Identify, formulate and solve engineering problems using hardware and / or software with a comprehensive approach applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze and synthesize hardware and software design processes and / or their integration, resulting in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Perform experimentation with hardware and / or software to establish conclusions from the data analysis and interpretation.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize the professional and ethical implications involved in the proposed solutions to understand their impact in a global, economic, environmental and social sphere.
  • Recognize the need for continuous updating to solve current problems in computer engineering.
  • Work responsibly and efficiently in teams during the development of hardware and / or software projects.

Educational objectives

  • The graduates identify, form and solve problems that require the analysis, design, integration, implementation and evaluation of computer systems.
  • Computer Engineers keep up to date to face and solve contemporary challenges through certifications, postgraduate studies, diplomas, courses, research projects and / or technological development considering social, economic and environmental aspects framed in the ethical and moral.
  • Computer Engineers will develop values in professional environments at a global level, communicating effectively orally and in writing with multidisciplinary and multicultural groups.

Occupational Field

Computer Engineer may work in:

Private sector:

  • Micro businesses
  • Industrial companies
  • Research centers

Public Sector:

  • Government Institutions
  • Communications and transport field
  • Educational and research Institutions
  • Research centers

Independent professionals:

  • Advising, designing, implementing, documenting and evaluating projects
  • Related with automation, computer networking and / or engineering software
  • Designing, selecting and installing hardware and software systems
  • Maintain computer systems in optimal conditions
  • Innovate and generate computer technologies
  • Marketing and promoting the use of computer systems
  • Design and execute training programs
