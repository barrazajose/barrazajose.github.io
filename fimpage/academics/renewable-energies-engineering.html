Renewable Energies Engineering Program

MISSION

To train competent human resources in the evaluation, diagnosis and planning of energy resources and development of renewable energy projects through relevant study plans that help the sustainable development of the country.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2025, the Renewable Energies educational program is one of the best in the country training renewable energy leaders in the dissemination of renewable energy sources, the generation of strategies for saving and the efficient use of energy resources which contribute to sustainability and the environment conservation.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admission Profile

Applicants to enter the Renewable energies educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics
  • Administration
  • Social and humanistic sciences

Skills:

  • Problem analysis
  • Document reading and writing
  • Summarizing information
  • Computer knowledge
  • Integrate in teams with organization and discipline

Attitudes:

  • Analytical and critical thinking
  • Initiative and Creativity
  • Proactive

Values:

  • Respect and appreciation for the environment
  • Responsibility
  • Tolerance
  • Collaborative
  • Honesty

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Identify, formulate and solve complex engineering problems applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze, and synthesize engineering design processes that result in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Develop and conduct adequate experimentation; analyze and interpret data using engineering judgment to draw conclusions.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize their ethical and professional responsibilities in relevant situations in order to make informed judgments considering the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and social context.
  • Recognize the permanent need for additional knowledge and have the ability to locate, evaluate, integrate and apply this knowledge appropriately.
  • Work effectively in teams to set goals, plan tasks, meet deadlines and analyze risks.

Educational objectives

  • Analyze, design and optimize systems for generation, saving and efficient use of energy.
  • Generate and analyze data.
  • Develop, propose and promote energy strategies.
  • Integrate and lead effective work teams.

Occupational Field

Renewable Energies Engineer may work in:

Private sector:

  • Government Institutions
  • Educational Institutions
  • Research Institutions
  • Public services

Public Sector:

  • Government institutions doing activities such as: diagnostic, evaluation, implementation and management to reach the optimization of energy resources.
  • Industrial companies
  • Educational and research Institutions

Independent professionals:

  • Advisory, consulting, training and project evaluation activities in different areas of the energy sector.

Main Projects

  1. Diagnosis and Energy Management in the Rio Colorado-Tijuana Aqueduct Pumping Plants.
  2. Problems of shortage and over generation in micro networks that incorporate renewable energies.
  3. Development of thermo-energy analysis methodologies to promote a culture of saving and efficient use of energy in the industrial sector of Mexicali, Baja California.
